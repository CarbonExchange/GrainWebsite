Few Words About Us

 

GRAIN is a new venture incubated at Greentown Labs, funded by Schneider Electric and accelerated by the Clean Tech Open Northeast and Micosoft Founders Hub.

We created GRAIN to address global warming and accelerate decarbonization.

We are focused on unlocking the Voluntary Carbon Offset Market with the goal to have a large impact on slowing climate change through simplifying and accelerating the Carbon Offset certification process.
